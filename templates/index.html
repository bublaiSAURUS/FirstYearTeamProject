How to Use?

01

StartSolving

Click on the 'Start Solving' button to start solving equations with Wolfram Beeta!

02

Choose Equations

Click the solver of your choice!

Linear

How to Solve?

- Enter the number of variables.
- Enter the coefficients of your choice separated by commas.
- Enter the constants of your choice also separated by commas.
- Follow the same steps for the Non-linear solver.

ODE

How to Solve?

- Enter the coefficients of your choice separated by commas.
- Depending on number of initial conditions, enter three conditions separated by commas.
- The first number of the condition will be the order of function.
- The second number will be the value of the x.
- The third number will be the constant value of the initial condition.
- Repeat this for the number of initial conditions.

PDE

How to Solve?

Enter the coefficients of your choice separated by commas and click solve!
