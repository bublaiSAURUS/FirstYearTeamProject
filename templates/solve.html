Linear Equations

A linear equation is an algebraic equation of the form y=mx+b. involving only a constant and a first-order (linear) term, where m is the slope and b is the y-intercept.

Non-linear Equations

A non-linear equation is such which does not form a straight line. It looks like a curve in a graph and has a variable slope value.

ODEs

An ordinary differential equation (ODE) is a differential equation whose unknown(s) consists of one (or more) function(s) of one variable and involves the derivatives of those functions.

PDEs

A partial differential equation (PDE) is an equation which computes a function between various partial derivatives of a multivariable function.
